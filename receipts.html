A personal message from the founder

You did not just spend
£1.78.

One Flame started as a question. What if we could show the people who make decisions, in a way they could not ignore, that the majority of the world simply wants peace? Not a petition. Not a protest. Something visible, living, growing in real time.

Every single person who lights a candle on this map makes that possible. You added your voice to something that did not exist before. You stood next to strangers from countries you may never visit, and said the same thing they said. Enough. We want something better.

The charity donations are not the point of this. They are a promise kept. When I said your flame would help others, I meant it, and the receipts on this page are the proof. Every pound sent to them came from this community. From you.

I do not know where One Flame goes from here. I know it started with a map of golden lights growing slowly from nothing. And I know that every one of those lights was a real person, making a real choice to believe that their voice matters.

It does. You do. Thank you for being part of this.

Michael C
Founder · oneflame.earth
