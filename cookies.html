Cookie Policy

Last updated: Easter 2026 · oneflame.earth

We keep this simple. We use a small number of cookies to make the site work and to understand how people find us. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. We never will.

What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file stored on your device when you visit a website. It helps the site remember things like your language preference or whether you have already seen this notice.

What cookies do we use?

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
oneflame_cookie_consent Essential Remembers whether you have accepted or declined cookies so we do not ask you again. 1 year
oneflame_lang Essential Remembers your language preference so the site loads in your language next time. 1 year
Stripe (_stripe_mid, _stripe_sid) Essential Set by Stripe when you make a payment. Required for secure transaction processing. We do not control these cookies. Session / 1 year
Analytics (if accepted) Analytics Helps us understand how many people visit the site and which countries they come from. No personal information is collected. Used only to improve the campaign. Up to 2 years

Your choices

You chose your preference when you first visited the site. You can change your mind at any time using the controls below. Choosing essential only means the site still works fully. You will just not contribute to our anonymous visitor count.

Your current cookie preference

Change your preference at any time. Your choice is saved immediately.

Third party cookies

When you make a payment, you are taken to Stripe's secure payment page at buy.stripe.com. Stripe sets their own cookies for fraud prevention and payment security. These are governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy.

We do not use Facebook Pixel, Google Ads, TikTok Pixel or any advertising tracking cookies on this site.

How to manage cookies in your browser

You can also control cookies directly through your browser settings. Every major browser lets you view, block or delete cookies. Here are the links for the most common browsers.

Changes to this policy

If we change how we use cookies we will update this page and change the date at the top. We will never introduce advertising or tracking cookies without clearly telling you first.
