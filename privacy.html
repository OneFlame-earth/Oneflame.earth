Privacy Policy

We keep this simple. Here is exactly what data we collect, why we collect it and how long we keep it.

Last updated: April 2026
01

Who we are

One Flame is operated by Michael C, a British national. For the purposes of UK and EU data protection law, Michael C is the data controller for personal data collected through this site.

Contact: [email protected]

Our approach in plain English: We collect only what we need. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with third parties except the services that power this site. Your candle data is deleted automatically after 7 days.

02

What data we collect

When you place a candle, we store the following in our database:

Data Why we collect it Retention
Your country and home town To place your candle in the right location on the map 7 days
Your nickname (optional) To display on the map and public ledger if you choose to share one 7 days
Your intention (optional) To attach meaning to your flame 7 days
Your flame code To link your candle to your payment and identify your flame 7 days
Stripe session ID To confirm payment was received 7 days
Approximate coordinates To render your dot on the world map 7 days

We do not collect your name, email address or any payment details directly. Your email and payment card are handled entirely by Stripe.

03

Payments and Stripe

All payments are processed by Stripe Payments Europe Ltd, an authorised payment institution regulated in Ireland. Stripe handles your card details, email address and billing information.

When you pay, Stripe may send you a receipt to the email you provide at checkout. That email is held by Stripe under their own privacy policy. We do not receive or store your payment card details at any point.

You can read Stripe's privacy policy at stripe.com/gb/privacy.

04

Data storage and security

Your candle data is stored in Supabase, a PostgreSQL database hosted on AWS infrastructure in the EU. Row Level Security is enabled, meaning public visitors can only read confirmed, non-expired candles. Nobody can read unconfirmed or expired records.

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages over HTTPS. All data transmitted between your browser and our services is encrypted in transit.

Your candle record is automatically deleted 7 days after your payment is confirmed. We do not archive, back up or retain expired candle data beyond that point.

05

Public data

The following information is publicly visible on this site to all visitors:

  • Your flame code
  • Your nickname (if you provided one)
  • Your country and home town
  • Your intention (if you provided one)
  • The approximate position of your candle on the map

Do not enter your full name, address or any other personal information in the nickname or hometown fields if you do not wish it to be publicly visible.

06

Cookies and analytics

We use essential cookies to support basic site functionality. We also use analytics cookies to understand how people find and use this site. No personal data is sold or shared with advertisers.

You can control your cookie preferences at any time. See our full Cookie Policy for details.

07

Your rights under UK GDPR

If you are based in the UK or EU, you have the following rights regarding any personal data we hold about you:

  • Right of access — you can ask what data we hold about you
  • Right to erasure — you can ask us to delete your candle record before the 7-day expiry
  • Right to rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to object — you can object to processing in certain circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, email us at [email protected] with your flame code. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are unhappy with how we handle your data, you have the right to complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

08

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at [email protected] and we will delete it promptly.

09

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. We will not make any changes that reduce your rights without clear notice.

10

Contact

For any privacy questions or to exercise your rights, write to us at [email protected].
