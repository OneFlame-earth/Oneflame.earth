Deductions — in order of priority

What comes out first.

Revenue from each candle purchase flows through the following deductions before charitable allocation. Each layer is shown here in full.

Item Detail Recipient Amount Status Link
1. Payment processing Stripe fee per transaction Stripe Inc, USA ~1.4% + 20p Deducted
2. Tax — HMRC NHS, Fire Brigade, Social Welfare, Security. Up to 20% retained for tax obligations, cashflow and infrastructure. HMRC, United Kingdom Up to 20% Obligation
3. Running costs Domain, database, hosting, maintenance, marketing Various <£2 / month Ongoing

After the above deductions, up to 80% of remaining net revenue is allocated to the charities below.
