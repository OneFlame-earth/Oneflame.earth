Important Notice · Financial Transparency

One Flame is an independent digital initiative and is not a registered charity. All figures shown on this page are based on Stripe payouts received and are updated within 48 hours of each monthly payout on the 15th.

Tax shown on this page is income tax paid personally by the founder to HMRC on revenue received. It is described here as a contribution to public services because that is what it is. The tax bracket applied is the standard UK income tax rate applicable at the time of each payout.

Milestone charity donations are voluntary commitments made personally by the founder. They are separate from operating costs and are paid directly to each charity when a flame milestone is reached. Equal distribution means every charity receives exactly the same amount regardless of size or profile.

Operating costs include but are not limited to: Stripe fees, GitHub Pages, Supabase, Namecheap domain, and any AI or software tools used to build and maintain the platform. Up to 20% of net revenue may be applied to these costs.

By lighting a candle on oneflame.earth you are purchasing a digital product. You are not making a charitable donation directly. The milestone donations described here are a voluntary commitment by the founder of One Flame.
