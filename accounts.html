Revenue

How we earn.

Each candle is purchased for £1.78. After Stripe’s payment processing fee (approximately 1.4% + 20p per UK card transaction), net revenue per candle is approximately £1.55.

How revenue is distributed

Where the money goes.

After payment processing fees and tax obligations (handled at up to 20% to cover cashflow, maintenance, marketing and infrastructure), up to 80% of net revenue after tax is allocated to selected global charities. The founder retains a portion for time and ongoing platform development. Full charity allocation is published on the charities page.
